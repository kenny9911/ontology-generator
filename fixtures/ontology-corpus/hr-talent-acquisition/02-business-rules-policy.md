## Compensation & Offer Rules

**R1.** An `Offer` may not be extended unless its linked `Requisition` is in status `Approved`. — *Source: Compensation Guidelines COMP-GL-2026 §2.1.*

**R2.** An `Offer` whose proposed base salary falls above the role's published `SalaryBand` maximum is required to obtain VP approval recorded against the `Requisition` before it may be extended. — *Source: Compensation Guidelines COMP-GL-2026 §2.3.*

**R3.** An in-band `Offer` shall be approved by both the Hiring Manager and the HR Business Partner before extension. — *Source: Compensation Guidelines COMP-GL-2026 §2.2.*

**R4.** A `Requisition` whose proposed annualized cost exceeds the funded `Position` budget by more than 10% must be escalated to VP approval before it may be published. — *Source: Recruiting Playbook RP-2026 §1.4.*

**R12.** A proposed base salary that is below the role's `SalaryBand` minimum may not be entered on an `Offer` without a documented Compensation Analyst exception. — *Source: Compensation Guidelines COMP-GL-2026 §2.5.*

**R13.** A sign-on bonus on an `Offer` may not exceed 20% of the proposed annual base salary without VP approval. — *Source: Compensation Guidelines COMP-GL-2026 §3.2.*

## Interview & Hiring Rules

**R5.** Every `Application` must clear a structured `InterviewPanel` of at least three `Interviewer`s before it may reach the `Offer` stage. — *Source: Interview & Hiring Policy IHP-2026 §4.1.*

**R6.** A hiring decision of `Advance to Offer` shall not be recorded until every assigned `Interviewer` has submitted a `Scorecard` for the `Application`. — *Source: Interview & Hiring Policy IHP-2026 §4.3.*

**R7.** For engineering roles, a `Candidate` whose `Assessment` score is below 70% may not advance past the `Recruiter Screen` stage. — *Source: Recruiting Playbook RP-2026 §3.2.*

**R8.** No single `Interviewer` may submit more than one `Scorecard` for the same `Application`. — *Source: Interview & Hiring Policy IHP-2026 §4.2.*

**R14.** At least one member of every `InterviewPanel` is required to be a trained Bar Raiser independent of the hiring team. — *Source: Interview & Hiring Policy IHP-2026 §4.4.*

**R15.** An `Interviewer` who has a personal relationship with the `Candidate` must recuse from the `InterviewPanel` and may not submit a `Scorecard`. — *Source: Interview & Hiring Policy IHP-2026 §6.1.*

## Eligibility, Timing & Compliance Rules

**R9.** An `Offer` is required to specify an expiry date no more than 7 business days after it is extended, and an `Offer` not accepted by its expiry date shall transition to status `Expired`. — *Source: Recruiting Playbook RP-2026 §5.1.*

**R10.** A `Candidate` may not be converted to a `Worker` until a `BackgroundCheck` with result `Clear` is on file; a result of `Consider` shall suspend the hire pending individualized adjudication. — *Source: Interview & Hiring Policy IHP-2026 §7.2.*

**R11.** A `Requisition` must remain open for a minimum posting period of 5 business days before any `Offer` may be extended against it. — *Source: Recruiting Playbook RP-2026 §1.6.*

**R16.** A `Candidate`'s personal data must be retained for no longer than 24 months after the final `Application` disposition unless the `Candidate` has consented to a longer talent-pool retention. — *Source: Interview & Hiring Policy IHP-2026 §8.3.*

**R17.** Every `Application` that is rejected is required to record a standardized disposition reason code before it may be closed. — *Source: Recruiting Playbook RP-2026 §4.5.*

**R18.** An internal `Candidate` who is a current employee shall be granted an interview for any `Requisition` for which they meet the minimum qualifications. — *Source: Recruiting Playbook RP-2026 §2.7.*

**R19.** A `Requisition` may not be reopened more than 12 months after it was closed; a new `Requisition` is required instead. — *Source: Recruiting Playbook RP-2026 §1.9.*

**R20.** An `Offer` to a `Candidate` for a regulated role may not be extended until an export-control screening has returned a `Cleared` result. — *Source: Interview & Hiring Policy IHP-2026 §7.5.*
